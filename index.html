Privacy Policy for AI Text Assistant (Beta)

Last Updated: June 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Text Assistant (Beta) ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information.

Data We Collect

Our extension collects the following types of data:

  • Personal Communications: We temporarily process the content of LinkedIn messages you are composing to provide AI-powered text suggestions.
  • Website Content: We collect information from LinkedIn pages, including profile information (your name, role, company, and headline) and recipient information (name and headline) to provide context for AI suggestions.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the purpose of providing AI-powered text assistance for LinkedIn messages. Specifically:

  • To generate contextually relevant text suggestions
  • To personalize message suggestions based on your profile and the recipient's information
  • To improve the functionality of the extension

Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with:

  • Google's Gemini AI API: We send your message content and contextual information to Google's Gemini AI API through our secure proxy server to generate text suggestions.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties for marketing purposes or any purposes unrelated to the extension's core functionality.

Data Storage

Most data is processed temporarily in memory and is not permanently stored. Limited profile information may be stored in your browser's local storage to improve performance but remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers for storage.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All communication with our servers and third-party APIs uses secure HTTPS connections
  • We use a proxy server to securely manage API authentication
  • We do not store your message content on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable the extension at any time
  • Clear your locally stored data by clearing your browser's local storage

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
